Our Mission

Makerspad is the cloud-based product intelligence platform designed for Product Managers. Makerspad helps Product Managers deliver flawless user experience across web, mobile and wearables by analyzing user behaviors and identifying root causes and business impacts. With features such as pattern recognition, anomaly detection, correlation analysis and predictive forecasting, Makerspad connects a user behavior data with code-level trace and infrastructure metrics to exactly pinpoint the issue, thus ensuring flawless experience both pre and post product launch.
